# Review design quality

## Trigger

Design spec or mockup needs quality review before engineering handoff.

## Workflow

1. Validate task flows against user goals.
2. Check consistency with design-system patterns.
3. Review accessibility, responsiveness, and edge states.
4. Identify ambiguity that could block implementation.
5. Provide prioritized improvements and handoff notes.

## Output

- Design quality findings
- Clarifications needed for handoff
- Prioritized improvement recommendations
